About This Tool

Not sure which lift to hit today? Let the Weight Lifting Randomizer decide. Whether you want to focus on a specific muscle group or just let fate pick your movement, this tool keeps your gym sessions fresh and unpredictable.

The Weight Lifting Randomizer draws from over 85 exercises across 7 categories: Chest, Back, Shoulders, Legs, Arms, Core, and Full Body / Olympic lifts. From the Barbell Bench Press to the Turkish Get-up, every roll gives you a quality movement to build around.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Random Exercise" to receive a random muscle group and a specific exercise within it. Use the re-roll buttons (↻) to swap just the muscle group or just the exercise. Use the options page to disable any categories or movements you don't have equipment for.
