About This Tool

Path of Exile 2 is poised to redefine the action RPG genre with its deep skill system and extensive character customization. As the successor to the famously complex Path of Exile, the sequel promises even more ways to build a unique character. The sheer number of options can be overwhelming for new players and veterans alike.

This build idea generator is designed to provide a spark of inspiration for your next journey into Wraeclast. By randomly selecting a class, ascendancy, weapon type, and primary skills, it presents you with a core concept to build around. This is a fantastic way to discover powerful new combinations and challenge yourself to master playstyles you might never have considered, ensuring no two playthroughs are the same.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Roll All" button to generate a complete build concept, including your class, ascendancy, weapon, and associated skills. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any part of the concept you'd like to tweak. Let this tool be the starting point for your next great Path of Exile 2 theorycraft.
