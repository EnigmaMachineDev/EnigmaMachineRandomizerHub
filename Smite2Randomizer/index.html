About This Tool

Smite 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the popular third-person MOBA, bringing enhanced graphics, refined gameplay mechanics, and a fresh roster of gods from mythology. As the game continues to grow in its open beta phase, players have access to a diverse selection of deities and game modes to master.

This randomizer is designed to help you explore the full breadth of Smite 2's offerings by randomly selecting a god, role, and game mode for your next match. Whether you're testing new strategies, learning different gods, or simply looking to add variety to your gameplay, this tool will keep your Smite 2 experience dynamic and engaging.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Roll All" button to generate a complete random selection including god, role, and game mode. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any specific aspect while keeping the others. Configure your preferences in the options page to exclude gods, roles, or game modes you don't want to play.
