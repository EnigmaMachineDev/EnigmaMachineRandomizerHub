About This Tool

The Dice Roller simulates rolling standard tabletop gaming dice, including all the common types used in Dungeons & Dragons and other RPGs. From the humble D4 to the mighty D100, this tool provides quick and fair dice rolls for your gaming sessions.

How to Use

Click any of the standard dice buttons (D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, D100) to roll that die once. For more complex rolls, use the custom roller to specify the number of dice and sides. For example, enter "3" dice with "6" sides to roll 3D6. The tool will show both the total and individual roll results.

Dice Types

  • D4: 4-sided die (tetrahedron)
  • D6: 6-sided die (standard cube)
  • D8: 8-sided die (octahedron)
  • D10: 10-sided die (pentagonal trapezohedron)
  • D12: 12-sided die (dodecahedron)
  • D20: 20-sided die (icosahedron)
  • D100: 100-sided die (percentile)
