About This Tool

Building a custom bass guitar involves choosing from dozens of tonewoods, pickup configurations, hardware options, and neck profiles. Whether you're planning a Warmoth build, a parts bass, or just daydreaming about your dream instrument, this randomizer generates unique bass guitar specifications to inspire your next project.

From classic Precision and Jazz style basses to modern multi-scale designs, every combination of body wood, pickup layout, and hardware creates a unique tonal character. Use this tool to explore combinations you might never have considered.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Build" button to get a completely random bass guitar specification. Use the individual re-roll buttons to change any specific component. Use the Configure Options page to customize which items can appear in your results.
