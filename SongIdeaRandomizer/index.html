About This Tool

Stuck in a creative rut? The Song Idea Randomizer generates random musical parameters to inspire your next composition. Whether you're a seasoned producer or just picking up an instrument, random constraints can spark unexpected creativity and push you into uncharted musical territory.

From BPM and time signature to key, genre, mood, chord progression, and song structure, every roll gives you a unique starting point. Use it as a strict challenge or just a loose guide — the choice is yours.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Song Idea" for a complete random song concept. Use individual re-roll buttons to change any specific parameter. Visit the Configure Options page to customize which genres, instruments, moods, and more can appear in your results.

Song Structure Legend

I = Intro | O = Outro | A/B/C/D/E/F = Main sections (you decide: verse, chorus, theme, drop, etc.) | P = Pre-section (pre-chorus, build-up) | T = Transition/Drop (post-chorus, drop, release) | S = Solo/Instrumental | X = Breakdown
