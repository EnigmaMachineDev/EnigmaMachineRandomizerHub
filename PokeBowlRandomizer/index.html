    About This Tool

    Welcome to the Poke Bowl Randomizer! Poke (pronounced poh-KAY) is a Hawaiian dish of seasoned raw fish served over rice, now popular worldwide as customizable "build-your-own" bowls. This tool helps you discover creative poke bowl combinations by randomly selecting a base, protein, sauce, toppings, crunch element, and an optional extra.

    Whether you're a poke enthusiast looking to try something new, or a restaurant owner seeking inspiration, this randomizer draws from authentic Hawaiian ingredients and modern poke fusion options. Supports dietary preferences with vegan and vegetarian modes for plant-based bowls.

    How to Use the Randomizer

    Click "Generate Bowl" to create a complete poke bowl combination. You'll get a base, protein, sauce, three toppings, a crunch element, and an extra. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to swap out specific components while keeping the rest of your bowl intact.

    Visit the options page to configure dietary modes and customize which ingredients are available for randomization.
