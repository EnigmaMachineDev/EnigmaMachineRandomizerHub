About This Tool

StarCraft: Brood War is the legendary real-time strategy game that defined competitive gaming. With three asymmetric races—Terran, Protoss, and Zerg—the game offers unparalleled strategic depth and endless tactical possibilities.

This randomizer helps you explore different strategies by randomly selecting a race and unit focus. Whether you're practicing for competitive play or just want to try something new, this tool will guide you to unexplored playstyles.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate All" to get a complete random setup with a race and unit focus. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific selections. Visit the options page to customize which races and unit types you want to include.
