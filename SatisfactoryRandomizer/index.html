About This Tool

Welcome to the Satisfactory Recipe Randomizer! This tool helps you discover new ways to build your factory by randomizing alternate recipes for production items.

Satisfactory features numerous alternate recipes that can be unlocked through Hard Drives. This randomizer selects one recipe variant for each item that has alternates, helping you plan unique factory layouts and optimize your production chains.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Randomize All Recipes" to generate a complete set of recipe choices for your factory. Each item will show the selected recipe with its inputs and outputs. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific recipes, or use the Configure Options page to enable/disable specific items.

The recipes shown include both standard and alternate variants. Alternate recipes often trade different resources or change production ratios, allowing for creative factory designs!
