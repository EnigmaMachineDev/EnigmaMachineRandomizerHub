About This Tool

Spending quality time with your infant is crucial for their development and bonding. With so many activities to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide what to do each day. This Baby Activity Randomizer helps parents and caregivers discover engaging activities across multiple developmental areas.

From sensory play and music to outdoor adventures and quiet time, this tool offers 150 carefully curated activities organized into 10 categories. Each activity is designed to support your baby's physical, cognitive, social, and emotional development while creating precious memories together.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Activity" button to receive a random activity suggestion from any category. You can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific selections. Use the options page to customize which categories and activities are included in the randomization, allowing you to focus on age-appropriate activities or your baby's current interests.
