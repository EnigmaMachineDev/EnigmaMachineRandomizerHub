About This Tool

StarCraft II is Blizzard Entertainment's legendary real-time strategy game featuring three distinct races: Terran, Protoss, and Zerg. Each race offers unique units, strategies, and playstyles that create endless tactical possibilities.

This randomizer helps you explore different unit compositions and strategies by randomly selecting a race and specific units to focus on. Whether you're practicing micro, trying new builds, or just looking for a fun challenge, this tool will keep your gameplay fresh and exciting.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate All" to get a complete random setup with a race and units. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific selections. Visit the options page to customize which races and units you want to include.
