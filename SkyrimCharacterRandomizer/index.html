      About This Tool

      The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is a legendary open-world RPG that has captivated players for over a decade. Its core appeal lies in the freedom to be who you want and do what you want. However, after countless hours in its world, many players find themselves defaulting to the same stealth archer build or replaying the same familiar quests.

      This character randomizer is designed to shatter those habits and breathe new life into your adventures. By generating a random race, skillset, and faction allegiance, it provides the foundation for a unique role-playing experience. Will you be a Khajiit brawler who joins the Stormcloaks? Or an Orcish mage loyal to the Imperial Legion? The possibilities are designed to challenge you and encourage you to engage with parts of the game you may have never touched before. The tool also includes starts for the popular "Alternate Start - Live Another Life" mod, as well as optionally the Additional Starts addon mod for it, providing an even more unique beginning to your story.

      How to Use the Randomizer

      Click "Generate Character" for a complete role-playing concept. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to adjust any element of the character you wish. If you use the "Additional Starts" addon mod for Alternate Start, check the box to include its starting scenarios in the randomization pool.
