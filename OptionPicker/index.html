Add Your Options

About This Tool

The Option Picker helps you make decisions by randomly selecting from a list of choices you provide. Perfect for choosing what to eat, which movie to watch, or any situation where you need help deciding between multiple options.

How to Use

Type an option in the input field and click "Add" or press Enter. Repeat to add multiple options. Once you have at least two options, click "Pick Random Option" to randomly select one. You can remove individual options by clicking the × button next to them, or clear all options at once.
