About This Tool

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt offers an incredibly deep RPG experience with countless ways to build Geralt. Between combat skills, signs, alchemy, mutations, and armor sets, it's easy to fall into the same playstyle every time. This randomizer is designed to shake things up and push you out of your comfort zone.

Generate a completely random build including your skill focus, mutation, armor set, sign priorities, and even major narrative choices. Whether you're a veteran witcher or starting a new playthrough, this tool will help you discover new strategies and experience the game in fresh ways. It also randomizes key story decisions to create unique narrative experiences.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Build" to get a complete random setup for your playthrough. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific aspects while keeping the rest. Configure which options are available in the Options page. Toss a coin to your randomizer!
