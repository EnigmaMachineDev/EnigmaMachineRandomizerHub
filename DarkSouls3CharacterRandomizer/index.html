    About This Tool

    Dark Souls 3, the final chapter in the iconic series by FromSoftware, is renowned for its challenging combat and deep character customization. With a vast arsenal of weapons and armor sets, players can craft countless builds. However, many players often gravitate towards the same tried-and-true setups.

    This character randomizer is designed to break that cycle. It encourages you to explore the full breadth of what Dark Souls 3 has to offer by generating unexpected and unique character builds. Whether you get a powerful greatsword you've never used or a challenging combination of rings and spells, this tool encourages you to adapt your playstyle and experience the Kingdom of Lothric in a new light. It's perfect for veteran players seeking a new challenge or anyone looking to spice up their next playthrough.

    How to Use the Randomizer

    Click the "Generate Character" button for a complete, randomized build. You can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) for any category you'd like to change. Each item links to its Fandom wiki page, so you can easily find details about its stats and in-game location.
