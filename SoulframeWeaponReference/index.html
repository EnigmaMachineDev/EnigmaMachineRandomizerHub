About This Tool

Welcome to the Soulframe Weapon Reference, your comprehensive database for all weapons in Soulframe! This tool provides a sortable and searchable table of all available weapons with their complete statistics and attributes.

Use the search bar to find specific weapons by name, or utilize the filter dropdowns to narrow down weapons by slot, art type, damage type, virtue, or rarity. Click on any column header to sort the table by that attribute.

How to Use the Reference

Click on any weapon name to view its full description and detailed statistics including damage values at different levels, special attacks, and virtue attunement caps. The table displays key statistics for quick comparison between weapons.

The filters can be combined to find exactly the weapon type you're looking for - for example, all Rare weapons that use Grace virtue, or all Sidearm weapons with Sharp damage.
