About This Tool

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is FromSoftware's critically acclaimed action-adventure game that challenges players with intense sword combat and multiple paths to victory. With four distinct endings and five unique skill trees, each playthrough can feel dramatically different depending on your choices.

This randomizer helps you discover new ways to experience Sekiro by randomly selecting an ending to pursue and a skill tree to focus on. Whether you're a veteran shinobi looking for a fresh challenge or planning your next playthrough, this tool will guide you toward unexplored combinations and playstyles.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Playthrough" button to receive a random ending goal and skill tree focus for your next Sekiro adventure. You can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific selections. Use the options page to customize which endings and skill trees are included in the randomization.
