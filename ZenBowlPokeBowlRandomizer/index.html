About This Tool

This randomizer generates a complete "Build Your Own" poke bowl order based on the actual menu from Zen Bowl, a poke restaurant in Charlotte, NC. It follows the restaurant's ordering steps: choose a base, pick 2 proteins, select 4 toppings, and pick a sauce.

Prices reflect the menu: Quinoa base adds +$2, Avocado adds +$1, Spicy Cucumber adds +$1, and Seaweed Salad adds +$1. Items marked with * may be raw or undercooked.

How to Use

Click "Randomize My Bowl" to generate a full bowl suggestion. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to re-randomize a specific step while keeping the others. Use ⚙️ Configure Options to enable or disable specific ingredients.

*Items may be raw or undercooked. Consuming raw or undercooked meat, poultry, seafood, shellfish, or eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illness.
