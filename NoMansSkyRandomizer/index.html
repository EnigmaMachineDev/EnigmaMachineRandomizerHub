About This Tool

No Man's Sky is an expansive space exploration game featuring infinite procedurally generated planets, diverse alien races, and countless ways to play. From peaceful exploration to piracy, from farming to bounty hunting, the universe offers endless opportunities.

This randomizer helps you discover new ways to play by randomly selecting activities and professions. Whether you're looking for a new money-making strategy or just want to try something different, this tool will guide you to unexplored gameplay styles.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Activity" to get a random profession or activity along with a race to roleplay. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific selections. Visit the options page to customize which activities and races you want to include.
