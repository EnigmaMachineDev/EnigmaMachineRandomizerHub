About This Tool

Minecraft is one of the most popular sandbox games in the world, offering endless possibilities for creativity, exploration, and survival. With multiple game modes, difficulty levels, and world generation options, each playthrough can feel completely unique.

This randomizer helps you break out of your comfort zone by randomly selecting game settings for your next Minecraft adventure. Whether you're a veteran player looking for a fresh challenge or someone who struggles to decide how to start a new world, this tool takes the guesswork out of the equation and gets you playing faster.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate All" button to instantly create a complete game setup with a random game mode, difficulty, and world type. If you want to change a specific setting, use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) next to each category.
