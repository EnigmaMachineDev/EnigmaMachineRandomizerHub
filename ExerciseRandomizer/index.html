About This Tool

Can't decide what type of workout to do today? Let this Exercise Randomizer make the call for you. Drawing from the full spectrum of exercise disciplines, it picks a random exercise from across Calisthenics, Cardio, and Weight Lifting to keep your training varied and spontaneous.

The General Exercise Randomizer pulls from a combined pool of 80 exercises across all three major training categories. Whether you end up doing a pistol squat, a fartlek run, or a barbell bench press, you're guaranteed a quality workout.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Random Exercise" to receive a random exercise category and specific exercise from the full pool. Use the re-roll buttons (↻) to change just the category or just the exercise. For more focused training, use the dedicated Calisthenics, Cardio, or Weight Lifting randomizers.
