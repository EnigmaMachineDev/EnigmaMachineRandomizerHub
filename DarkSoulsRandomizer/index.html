    About This Tool

    Dark Souls, the legendary action RPG from FromSoftware, offers incredible build diversity through its weapons, armor, and magic systems. This randomizer helps you break out of your comfort zone and discover new playstyles.

    This tool generates random character builds including weapons, armor sets, and spells. It's perfect for challenge runs, discovering new synergies, or simply adding variety to your playthroughs through Lordran.

    How to Use the Randomizer

    Click "Generate Character" to get a complete build. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to fine-tune specific parts of your loadout. Each item links to its wiki page for stats and location details.
