      About This Tool

      Hot sauce making is an art that combines heat, flavor, and creativity. With countless pepper varieties, vegetables, and fermentation techniques, the possibilities are endless. However, it's easy to fall into the habit of making the same recipes over and over.

      This hot sauce randomizer is designed to inspire new flavor combinations and push your hot sauce making skills in exciting directions. Whether you're a seasoned sauce maker or just starting out, this tool will generate unique recipes that challenge you to explore new ingredients and techniques. From mild and tangy to scorching hot, each randomized recipe offers a new adventure in flavor.

      How to Use the Randomizer

      Click the "Generate Recipe" button for a complete hot sauce recipe with peppers, vegetables, and fermentation method. You can use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change any category. Each ingredient links to its Wikipedia page for more information about flavor profiles and characteristics.
