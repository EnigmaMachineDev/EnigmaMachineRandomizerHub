About This Tool

Welcome to the Cooking Randomizer, your culinary inspiration generator! This tool helps you discover new meal combinations by randomly selecting a cooking method, protein, vegetable, and two additional sides from various categories including dairy, fruits, grains, and nuts.

Perfect for breaking out of cooking ruts, meal planning, or challenging yourself with new ingredient combinations. The randomizer features dietary mode options including vegan, vegetarian, and carnivore modes to match your dietary preferences.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click "Generate Meal" to create a complete meal combination. The randomizer will always give you a protein and vegetable, plus two random sides from other categories. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to change specific components while keeping the rest of your meal intact.

Visit the options page to configure dietary modes and customize which ingredients are available for randomization.
