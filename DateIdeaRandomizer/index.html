About This Tool

Welcome to the Date Idea Randomizer! Whether you're planning your first date, celebrating an anniversary, or just looking to break out of your routine, this tool offers over 200 creative date ideas across 10 different categories.

Categories Include

  • Creative & Artistic - Pottery classes, painting, crafts, and DIY projects
  • Outdoor & Adventure - Hiking, kayaking, stargazing, and camping
  • Food & Cooking - Cooking classes, restaurant hopping, and themed dinners
  • At-Home & Cozy - Game nights, movie marathons, and spa nights
  • Entertainment & Shows - Comedy shows, concerts, and live performances
  • Active & Competitive - Mini golf, bowling, escape rooms, and sports
  • Romantic & Intimate - Couples massages, sunset cruises, and special memories
  • Cultural & Educational - Museums, galleries, and exploring new neighborhoods
  • Unique & Quirky - Fortune telling, scavenger hunts, and volunteering
  • Seasonal Activities - Pumpkin carving, campfires, and seasonal fun

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Date Idea" button to get a random date suggestion from any category. Use the individual re-roll button (↻) to get a new idea. Visit the options page to customize which categories and specific date ideas you want to include in your randomization!
