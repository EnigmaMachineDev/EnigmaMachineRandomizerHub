      About This Tool

      Welcome to the Meal Prep Bowl Randomizer! This tool helps you create balanced, nutritious meal prep bowls by randomly selecting a base, protein, vegetables, sauce, toppings, crunch element, and an optional extra. Perfect for weekly meal planning or discovering new flavor combinations.

      Whether you're a meal prep enthusiast, fitness-focused, or just looking to eat healthier, this randomizer draws from a wide variety of grains, proteins, and vegetables to create customizable bowls. Supports dietary preferences with vegan, vegetarian, and pescatarian modes.

      How to Use the Randomizer

      Click "Generate Bowl" to create a complete meal prep bowl combination. You'll get a base, protein, three vegetables, a sauce/dressing, two toppings, a crunch element, and an extra. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to swap out specific components while keeping the rest of your bowl intact.

      Visit the options page to configure dietary modes and customize which ingredients are available for randomization.
