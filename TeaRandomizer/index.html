About This Tool

Welcome to the Tea Randomizer, your ultimate companion for discovering new teas from around the world! Whether you're a tea enthusiast, a beginner looking to explore different varieties, or someone seeking the perfect brew for any occasion, this tool will introduce you to a diverse world of tea.

This comprehensive randomizer covers everything from delicate green teas and robust black teas, to complex oolongs, subtle white teas, earthy pu-erh, soothing herbal infusions, and unique specialty blends. Each tea includes detailed information about its origin, flavor profile, brewing temperature, steeping time, caffeine content, ingredients, and step-by-step brewing instructions.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Tea" button to get a random tea from any category. Use the individual re-roll button (↻) to get a new selection. Each tea includes brewing parameters, ingredients with measurements, and detailed instructions to help you brew the perfect cup!
