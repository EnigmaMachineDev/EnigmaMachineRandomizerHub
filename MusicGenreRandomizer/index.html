About This Tool

Welcome to the Music Genre Randomizer! Whether you're looking to discover new music, challenge yourself as a musician, or just explore the vast world of musical styles, this tool offers 25+ major genres and over 500 subgenres to explore.

This randomizer is perfect for breaking out of your musical comfort zone, finding inspiration for creative projects, or simply discovering styles you've never heard before. From classic rock to experimental electronic, from traditional folk to modern hip-hop, there's a whole world of sound waiting to be explored.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Music Genre" button to get a random genre and subgenre combination. Use the individual re-roll buttons (↻) to randomize just the genre or just the subgenre. Perfect for music discovery, creative challenges, or expanding your musical horizons!
