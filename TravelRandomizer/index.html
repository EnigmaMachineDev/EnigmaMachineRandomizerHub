About This Tool

Welcome to the Travel Randomizer! Let fate decide your next adventure destination. This tool randomly selects a country from around the world and provides current US State Department travel advisory information.

Travel Advisory Levels Explained

Travel advisory levels are based on official US State Department data and indicate safety considerations for travelers. The Department reviews these advisories regularly to ensure accuracy.

Level 1: Exercise Normal Precautions

This is the lowest advisory level for safety and security risks. There is some risk in any international travel, and conditions in other countries may differ from those in the United States. Standard travel safety measures apply.

Level 2: Exercise Increased Caution

Be aware of increased risks to safety and security. Specific risks are described in the Travel Advisory. Travelers should stay informed about local conditions and take appropriate precautions.

Level 3: Reconsider Travel

Reconsider your travel to the destination due to serious risks to safety and security. Specific risks are described in the Travel Advisory. The U.S. government may have limited ability to provide emergency services to U.S. citizens in these locations.

Level 4: Do Not Travel

This is the highest advisory level due to life-threatening risks. Specific risks are described in the Travel Advisory. The U.S. government may have very limited or no ability to help, including during an emergency. We advise that U.S. citizens do not travel to the country, or to leave as soon as it is safe to do so.

How to Use the Randomizer

Click the "Generate Destination" button to get a random country with its travel advisory information. Use the individual re-roll button (↻) to select a different country. Always verify current travel advisories at travel.state.gov before making travel plans!
